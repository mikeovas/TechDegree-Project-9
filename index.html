About Me

I was born and raised in Toronto, Ontario and currently live in the nearby city of Mississauga. I love to hike and take nature pictures which has gotten me back into photography.

I always enjoyed the sciences and decided to pursue a career in it. I am a Chemical Engineering Technologist at a local college. My science background includes a Bachelors of Science Honors' Degree in Microbiology, and an Advanced Diploma in Chemical Engineering Technology.

My Background in Web Development

I have been interested in web design for a number of years and I am working to develop my skillset in web design so I can become a successful new web designer and become proficient in creating web design projects.

I have started with the Front End Web Development TechDegree Program offered by Treehouse and will continue with Back End Web Development afterwards. I have been able to increase my knowledge base and skills in: HTML, CSS & Javascript. This portfolio page was designed to showcase what I have learned.

My goal is to be able to do Front End and Back End Web Development on a more regular basis. I would like to set up a freelance, part-time business as I become more proficient.
